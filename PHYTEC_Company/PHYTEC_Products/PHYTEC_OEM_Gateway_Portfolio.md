# PHYTEC – OEM Gateway Portfolio (phyGATE®)

## 1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines PHYTEC’s **OEM gateway portfolio**, marketed under the **phyGATE®** product line.

It establishes how PHYTEC gateways should be:
- Positioned against SBCs and SoMs
- Recommended in IIoT and Industrial Edge AI scenarios
- Understood as **series‑ready OEM products**, not prototypes

---

## 2. What PHYTEC OEM Gateways Are

PHYTEC OEM gateways are **finished, industrial devices** designed for:

- IIoT and Industry 4.0 deployments
- Retrofit of existing machines
- Industrial data aggregation and protocol conversion
- Edge computing and Edge AI execution

They are delivered as **OEM products**, meaning:
- Hardware is production‑ready
- Electronics and software can be customized
- Housing, branding, and labeling can be adapted
- Devices are suitable for series production [1](https://www.phytec.eu/en/produkte/fertige-geraete-oem/)

---

## 3. Role of phyGATE in the PHYTEC Portfolio

The phyGATE product line fills a distinct role:

> **phyGATE = Ready‑to‑deploy industrial gateway platform**

It complements:
- **phyCORE / phyFLEX SoMs** (custom embedded products)
- **phyBOARD SBCs** (reference platforms and low‑volume systems)

phyGATE removes the need to design:
- Custom hardware
- Enclosures
- Power and EMC‑qualified systems

---

## 4. Core Characteristics of phyGATE OEM Gateways

PHYTEC phyGATE gateways share these core attributes:

- Industrial‑grade hardware design
- Long‑term availability
- DIN‑rail or industrial housing
- Broad industrial interfaces (Ethernet, CAN, RS‑485, IOs)
- Linux‑based operating system
- Prepared security features (e.g. TPM, secure boot)
- Professional update and device management readiness [1](https://www.phytec.eu/en/produkte/fertige-geraete-oem/)
