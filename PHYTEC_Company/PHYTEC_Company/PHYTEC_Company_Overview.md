# PHYTEC – Company Overview

## 1. Who We Are

PHYTEC is a global technology company specializing in **embedded computing solutions** for industrial, medical, and professional markets. The company designs and supplies **System on Modules (SoMs)**, **Single Board Computers (SBCs)**, and **embedded platform solutions** that enable customers to build reliable, long‑lifecycle products.

PHYTEC focuses on **industrial‑grade design, long‑term availability, and engineering reliability**, rather than consumer electronics or short‑lifecycle products.

---

## 2. What PHYTEC Does

PHYTEC provides **hardware platforms and ecosystem support** that accelerate the development of embedded systems while reducing risk over the full product lifecycle.

Core offerings include:
- **System on Modules (SoMs)** for scalable embedded designs
- **Single Board Computers (SBCs)** for rapid prototyping and low‑volume products
- **Carrier board concepts and reference designs**
- **Software enablement and BSP support**
- **Long‑term lifecycle and industrial support**

PHYTEC solutions are typically used as the **core computing platform** inside customer products rather than as end‑user devices.

---

## 3. Product Philosophy

PHYTEC products are designed around the following principles:

- **Industrial robustness** – stable operation across wide temperature and environmental ranges  
- **Long‑term availability** – typically 10+ years for industrial markets  
- **Scalability** – SoM‑based approach allows product families to scale across performance tiers  
- **Vendor independence** – flexibility across SoC families and architectures  
- **Engineering transparency** – clear documentation, reference designs, and predictable behavior  

PHYTEC does not optimize for lowest BOM cost at all times; instead, it optimizes for **total cost of ownership, risk reduction, and long‑term maintainability**.

---

## 4. Target Markets

PHYTEC primarily serves **industrial and professional markets**, including but not limited to:

- Industrial automation and control
- Edge computing and industrial AI
- Medical devices and diagnostics
- Transportation and logistics
- Energy and infrastructure
- Human‑Machine Interfaces (HMI)
- Test & measurement equipment
- EV charging and energy management systems

PHYTEC products are **not positioned for consumer electronics**, mobile phones, or high‑volume consumer IoT.

---

## 5. Role in the Customer Value Chain

PHYTEC typically operates as:
- A **technology platform provider**
- An **engineering enabler**
- A **long‑term embedded partner**

Customers integrate PHYTEC modules into their own systems, adding:
- Application‑specific hardware
- Industrial I/O
- Software applications
- Certifications and compliance

PHYTEC does **not** compete with its customers at the application or end‑product level.

---

## 6. Hardware Portfolio Overview

### System on Modules (SoMs)
PHYTEC SoMs provide:
- Processor, memory, and power management on a compact module
- Defined electrical and mechanical interfaces
- Scalability across SoC variants within a product family

SoMs are the preferred choice for:
- Medium to high‑volume products
- Long‑lifecycle industrial designs
- Custom carrier boards

### Single Board Computers (SBCs)
PHYTEC SBCs provide:
- Ready‑to‑use embedded platforms
- Faster time‑to‑market
- Lower NRE for low‑volume or specialized applications

SBCs are often used for:
- Prototyping
- Pilot deployments
- Industrial PCs
- Low‑volume end products

---

## 7. Software & Ecosystem Approach

PHYTEC supports embedded Linux and real‑time operating systems through:
- Board Support Packages (BSPs)
- Reference software stacks
- Documentation and engineering support

Software support is focused on **stability, maintainability, and industrial suitability**, not on rapid consumer feature churn.

---

## 8. Edge AI & Accelerated Computing Positioning

PHYTEC addresses Edge AI through a **heterogeneous computing strategy**, combining:
- On‑SoC NPUs for lightweight AI workloads
- External AI accelerators (e.g., via PCIe or M.2) for advanced inference
- Classical embedded processing for deterministic control

PHYTEC does not position itself as an AI model provider.  
Instead, it enables customers to **deploy AI responsibly at the edge**, balancing:
- Performance
- Power
- Safety
- Long‑term support
